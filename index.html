About Us

SkolaKuys is an online course platform that provides learning services about professions in the data field. This course was established in 2020 which was initiated by several employees who work in the field of data and final year students who basically want to share their knowledge with the general public. Through this course, the output we expect from our graduates is to be able to compete in startups and other agencies in this data field.
There are several advantages of studying at Skolakuys, including study time outside working hours, there are live classes 2 times a week, access to materials forever, and several other benefits that you can get. Skolakuys also offers study now, pay later where you can start studying by paying on credit. The material we teach is not only about hard skills such as data cleaning, SQL, Python, but we also teach soft skills such as how to turn the data into a story.
